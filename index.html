Enter your cards in the boxes below.
For numbered cards, enter the number (2 through 10 allowed).
For face cards, enter "A" for Ace, "K" for King, "Q" for Queen, "Ja" for Jack, "Jo" for Joker.

Goal is to get the lowest score.
Numbered cards are worth their value.
Ace: 1 point. King: 10 points. Queen: 10 points. Jack: 10 points. Joker: -5 points.
Pairs cancel out to zero, except Joker pairs which are worth -10 points.
